Rental Summary

We provide the opportunity of rental scoots on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. It is friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs for your special day.
The security deposit is $50 that is required to confirm reservations. A valid drivers license is required in hand at time of rental. Reservations rate requires 24 hour prior notification.
