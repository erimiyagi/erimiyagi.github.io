Top Cozumel Tours

Dolphin Swim Experience

An interesting activity you can find in the island is swimming with dolphins; you can do this at Dolphin Discovery-Cozumel. With this program, you’ll be able to interact with two dolphins in their natural environment. You’ll be able to touch, hug and kiss a dolphin, moreover, you’ll be able to feel the rush of being foot pushed by the dolphins to rise above the water.


Price: $100-150

Schedule: TUE, WED, THU, FRI, SAT


Tulum Mayan Ruins Express

Meet your tour operator at a pier in downtown Cozumel, where you board a ferry bound for mainland Mexico. After a pleasant ride with coastal views rarely seen by visitors, arrive in Playa del Carmen. An air-conditioned minivan brings you directly to the gates of Tulum. This tour includes lunch, water bottle, and a professional guide.


Price: $75

Suggested Duration: More than 6 hours


Zip Lines

Fly through the air on a series of zip lines at the exclusive ocean side Islands Beach Club! A short taxi ride brings you to the private beach club. Next receive your snorkel gear including mask, fins, and floatation vest. Jump into the aquamarine waters and snorkel amongst clouds of colorful fish.


Price: $89.99 / Adult (ages 13 and over)

$69.99 / Child (ages 12 and under)

Paradise Beach

Whether you want to relax in the shade or have fun in the sun, you've come to the right place! There's plenty here to please everyone! Gorgeous beach and huge, Vegas-style fresh/heated pool!


Price: $3 for a lounge chair
$10 food or drink minimum in or Traditional a la carte service

Suggested Duration: More than 3 hours
