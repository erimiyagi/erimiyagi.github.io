Preston Information

Our parade is each night, Thursday, Friday and Saturday at 6:30p.m. Line up is at the 2nd North and 1st West. Thursday, Friday and Saturday's parade are the traditional "bands and floats" parades. We encourage float entries, horses, and horse drawn outfits. Themes: Thursday is “Tough Enough to Wear Pink Night”, Friday is “Patriot Night” and Saturday is “Celebrate Cowboy Night”.
